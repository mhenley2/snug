Unlike any other venue in Bristol, The Snug is a quirky venue for anyone looking for an escape from the typical pub scene. Inside find our independent cafe and bookstore with a stage hosting open mic's and much more! Our grand opening is on March 15th so be sure to save the date.

What's on

The Snug is proud to host a great variety of events. From our vintage boardgames to our poetry slam there will always be something to peak your interest. Whether you're looking for a good night out or just a good book, The Snug will have everything you're looking for.

Where are we?

Situated in the heart of Bristol, The Snug is short walk or bus ride from anywhere in Bristol. This area is a vibrant and unique hub of life that captures the esssense of what Bristol's all about! Find us everyday from 4pm-4am at 32 St Nicholas St, Bristol BS1 1TG.

Poetry Slam

Our one of a kind poetry slams are held every Monday from 4pm.
Anyone is welcome to participate or watch as this night is always the highlight of our week.
A blend of proffesional acts and amateur means that there is no judgement and anyone can express themselves freely.

Board game library

Not only is our bookstore stocked with books and magazines, you can reserve and check out our vintage board games.

If you would like to rent a game simply fill out the form and email it back to maggie@thesnug.com.

Also available

The snug also sells stationary and art supplies in our bookstore. All of these items have been made from recyled parts so are eco-friendly!
Students can aslo get upto 25% off stationary, all you need to do is show your student ID!
